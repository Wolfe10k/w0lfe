FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before reaching out.

Do you work on Macs?

+
Yes — I work on Mac, Windows, Linux and everything in between. MacOS diagnostics, software issues, performance tuning and more.

How does the No Fix No Pay guarantee work?

+
Simple — if I can't solve your problem, you pay nothing. No hidden fees, no diagnostic charges, nothing. I only get paid when your issue is fully resolved.

How far do you travel for in-person jobs?

+
I'm based in LA and serve the greater Los Angeles area including South Central, Inglewood, Compton, Long Beach, Hawthorne, Torrance, Culver City, East LA, Downtown, Hollywood, Burbank, Pasadena and surrounding cities. For remote help I work with anyone in the US.

How long does a typical repair take?

+
Most software issues like virus removal, PC optimization and WiFi fixes are resolved in 1-2 hours. Hardware builds or complex business setups may take longer. I'll always give you an honest time estimate upfront.

Do you do remote support?

+
Yes — I can connect to your computer securely from anywhere in the US. You see everything I do in real time. Perfect for software issues, virus removal, slow PC fixes, and setup help.

What payment methods do you accept?

+
Cash, Venmo, Zelle, and CashApp. Payment is only collected after the job is done and you're satisfied.

Do you work on gaming PCs and setups?

+
Yes — I have competitive experience in CS2, Valorant, Overwatch, Rainbow Six, CoD, Mortal Kombat and more. I know exactly what settings and tweaks actually matter for competitive play. I'll optimize your drivers, Windows settings, network, polling rates, in-game configs, and monitor settings to squeeze out every possible edge.

Can you help set up a music production studio?

+
Yes — I produce music myself using Ableton and FL Studio. I can help set up your DAW, configure audio interfaces, route MIDI, calibrate studio monitors, and optimize your PC for low-latency audio production.

Do you offer business IT support?

+
Yes — POS systems, business WiFi, workstation setup, automations, and ongoing IT support. Monthly retainer packages are available for businesses that need regular support.

Can you fix my appliances?

+
Yes — refrigerators, washers, dryers and more from brands like Frigidaire, Whirlpool and Samsung. You'll need to provide the parts, I provide the labor and expertise.

Why are your prices so much lower than competitors?

+
I do this because I love it, not to maximize profit. I remember what it felt like to need help and not be able to afford it. Lower prices means more people get the help they actually need — that matters more to me than charging what the market will bear.

Can you optimize my PC specifically for competitive gaming?

+
Yes — this is one of my specialties. As a professional CS player I know exactly what settings, tweaks and configurations actually matter for competitive play. I'll optimize your drivers, Windows settings, network, polling rates, in-game configs, and monitor settings to get every possible edge.

Can you set up a Discord server for my community or business?

+
Absolutely — I can build your Discord server from scratch or clean up an existing one. Channels, roles, permissions, bots, verification, welcome messages, moderation systems — everything set up properly so your community runs smoothly.
